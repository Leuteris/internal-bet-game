Upstream 2020 EURO Cup Challenge – Terms and Conditions
  • Upstream is running an internal competition, called “2020 EURO Cup Challenge”.
  • The Competition will run from Tuesday 8/06 until the end of the official EURO Cup 2020.

Eligibility
  • Eligible to enter are only Upstream employees. The only requirement is to enter the competition with a corporate email address.

By entering the Quiz, participants agree to be bound by these rules.

How to enter
  • Participants should follow the instructions given on the relevant slack post and email.
  • Only one (1) entry per person is permitted. If someone submits more than one (1) entry, then only the first one will be taken into account.
  • Participants must send an email to the organisers (Dimitris Koukis) via their corporate email address asking to join the competition.
  • A unique account is created for each participant, sent to them by the organisers.

How to play
  • Picks will be provided by the players in a platform, and will be anonymous until the deadline (when all the picks will become public).
  • Results will be available on the same platform where you will be able to see what everyone played, their points, etc.
  • There will be specific deadlines for placing your predictions communicated via slack and/or email; in case you lose the date, you lose any opportunity to win points from that phase.
  • Participants do not enter any money in order to place their predictions.
  • Participants will make picks in five (5) phases (therefore predictions will be in chunks).
    • Group Stage. First match is on Friday, June 11;
    • Round of 16.
    • Quarter Finals.
    • Semi Finals.
    • Finals

Prizes

  • The prizes are as follows:

    • 7th prize: 3% of the total prize

    • 9th prize: 2% of the total prize
    • 10th prize: 2% of the total prize
  • There is also an 11th prize. The participant who will come last will also get a prize of 5% of the total prize. The participant must have participated in all phases to be eligible for the prize.
  • The prizes will be given as vouchers

Selection of the first 10 winners
  • The 10 winners will be those who have achieved the higher points at the end of the competition.
  • The points system is as follows:
  • Users select 1/X/2 in all matches (not the exact score).
    • If you miss the pick, you get 0 points.
    • If you make the pick, you get the "base points" multiplied by the "odds factor".
      • What is "base points"? Base points = 100 for all Group Stage matches, 200 for all the rest of the matches except for the Final that gives 300.
      • What are the "odds factor"? It's the factor rewarding risky predictions vs safe ones? Example:
        • Let's say England vs Italy gives 1.5 for "1", 2.5 for "X" and 3.5 for "2".
          • If you've selected "1" and you guessed it right you'll get Base Points * 1.5
          • If you've selected "X" and you guessed it right you'll get Base Points * 2.5
          • If you've selected "2" and you guessed it right you'll get Base Points * 3.5
          • Again: if you've guessed it wrong you get 0.
      • Odds will be based on real ones from a betting house that will be communicated.
  • Users will also select Over/ Under in all matches except for the Group Stage.
    • This gives extra points ONLY if the 1/X/2 pick was correct
    • Extra points given by guessing right is: 0.5 * Base Points * "O/U odds factor"
      • Yes, from a betting house
  • Note: All results are "90-min period" results (any overtime will be recorded as a tie = "X"); applies both on 1/X/2 and O/U
  • Most points win!
  • Winners will be announced upon the end of the competition via a slack message on the general channel and will also be notified individually.
  • The winners have five (5) business days upon being notified to accept their prize (mail or slack notification suffices). If a winner does not accept their prize, then this will be awarded to the next in line entrant as per correct answers and time taken. After the 5-day period, winner announcements will be final and binding on all matters relating to prize giveaways.
